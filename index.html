EZ RTC

  1. Create an offer on the 1st browser
  2. Copy offer and send to the 2nd browser
  3. Paste offer in 2nd browser and click Join
  4. Create an answer on the 2nd browser
  5. Copy answer and send to the 1st browser
  6. Paste answer in 1st browser and click Join
  7. Messages can now be sent between browsers
